# Self-Membership validated writes

Validated write permission to enable updating membership of a group in terms of adding/removing one's own account.



| Entry | Value |
|--------------|--------------------------------------|
| CN           | Self-Membership                      |
| Display-Name | Add/Remove self as member            |
| Rights-GUID  | bf9679c0-0de6-11d0-a285-00aa003049e2 |

# Recalculate-Hierarchy extended right

Extended right to force the DS to recalculate the hierarchy.

|--------------|--------------------------------------|
| CN           | Recalculate-Hierarchy                |

| Rights-GUID  | 0bc1554e-0a99-11d1-adbb-00c04fd8d5cd |

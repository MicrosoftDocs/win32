# XMBYTEN2 constructors

Constructors exposed by `XMBYTEN2`.

Constructs instances of the [**XMBYTEN2**](/windows/desktop/api/DirectXPackedVector/ns-directxpackedvector-xmbyten2) structure.

> [!Note]  
> The constructors listed here are only available with C++.

# PixelAspectRatioX

The **PixelAspectRatioX** attribute is the horizontal part of the aspect ratio of a video.

## Applies To

-   [Video Items](video-item-attributes.md)

## Remarks

For a video having an aspect ratio of 16:9, **PixelAspectRatioX** would equal "16".

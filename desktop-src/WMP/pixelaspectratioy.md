# PixelAspectRatioY

The **PixelAspectRatioY** attribute is the vertical part of the aspect ratio of a video.

## Applies To

-   [Video Items](video-item-attributes.md)

## Remarks

For a video having an aspect ratio of 16:9, **PixelAspectRatioY** would equal "9".

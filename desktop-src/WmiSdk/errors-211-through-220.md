## Fatal Error 220

<dl> <dt>

<span id="_220__Fatal____fileName___line____OBJECT-TYPE_invocation_of_SNMPv2SMI_not_allowed_"></span><span id="_220__fatal____filename___line____object-type_invocation_of_snmpv2smi_not_allowed_"></span><span id="_220__FATAL____FILENAME___LINE____OBJECT-TYPE_INVOCATION_OF_SNMPV2SMI_NOT_ALLOWED_"></span>**<220, Fatal>:"&lt;fileName&gt;:<line\#>: OBJECT-TYPE invocation of SNMPv2SMI not allowed"**
</dt> <dd>

</dd> </dl>

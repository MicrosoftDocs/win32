<span id="upnp.u_1_gly"></span><span id="UPNP.U_1_GLY"></span>**UDN**
</dt> <dd>

Stands for Unique Device Name. A UDN uniquely identifies a device; it is based on the UUID (universal unique ID).

</dd> <dt>

<span id="upnp.u_2_gly"></span><span id="UPNP.U_2_GLY"></span>**UTL**
</dt> <dd>

Stands for UPnP Template Language. The UPnP template language is used to specify device or service descriptions.
